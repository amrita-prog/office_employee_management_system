About This Project

A complete Office Employee Management System built with Django & Bootstrap 5

The Office Employee Management System (OEMS) is a web-based solution designed to simplify HR and employee workflows. It automates daily operations like employee records, attendance, leave management, and internal communication.

Objectives

  • Centralized employee data management
  • Track attendance, leaves, and performance efficiently
  • Enhance communication between employees and management
  • Automate payroll and reporting processes

Key Features

  • Employee Management
  • Attendance & Leave Tracking
  • Internal Communication
  • Performance & Task Management
  • Payroll Management
  • Document Handling
  • Reports & Analytics
  • Role-Based Access Control

Technologies Used

Backend: Python (Django)
Frontend: HTML, CSS, Bootstrap 5
Database: SQLite / MySQL
Authentication: Django Auth

This project was built to modernize HR workflows and improve office productivity.
